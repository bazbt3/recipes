## Roasted sprouts    
(Courtesy of @ellemef - thanks!)

### Ingredients:

* Sprouts, halved or quartered dependent on size,
* Olive oil,
* Salt.


### Preparation:

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (around 200°C),
2. Toss with the olive oil,
3. Spread on a baking sheet,
4. Roast for 30-40 minutes,
5. Salt & serve.
